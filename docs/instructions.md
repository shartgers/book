# Writing Instructions

Must-have instructions and guidelines for writing this book. All agents must follow this file. You may add further ideas or suggestions here; they will be treated as required for drafting and review.

---

## Named Models

The book introduces several models with **specific names**. Use these exact names and element lists in prose, beat sheets, research briefs, and diagrams. Do not rename or reorder the elements.

### 1. The AI Transformation Framework

**Pillars (five):** Product, People, Process, Technology, Data.

Use when describing what the Agentic Organisation is made of or how to assess organisational readiness. Introduced in Chapter 5 (The AI Transformation Framework).

### 2. The Four Tiers of Transformation

**Tiers (four):** Individual → Team → Department → Organisation (cross-functional).

Use when describing how transformation scales: from one person to teams, then departments, then the whole organisation. Introduced in Chapter 4 (The Four Tiers of Transformation).

### 3. The AI Decision Framework

**Elements (three):** Value, Capabilities, Trust.

Use when describing how to evaluate AI investments and set priorities. Introduced in Chapter 8 (Value, Capabilities, Trust).

## Chapter 10: What the Leaders Did

**Structure:** Nine **lessons** (abstracted during the research phase), with references to case studies where needed. The chapter is **lesson-led**, not case-study-led: each section is a transferable lesson; case material (BBVA, ING, Siemens, Allianz, Klarna, Schneider Electric) supports the lessons. Research Agent must abstract and synthesise the 9 lessons from case material and present them in the research brief. Planner: for this chapter only, Beat 4 is not a single "Case Study" slot; the beats should structure the chapter around the 9 lessons (e.g. Beat 4 = "Nine lessons — each with lesson statement and case references"). Writer structures the chapter around the lessons, citing cases as evidence or illustration. The Case Study Agent is not used for Chapter 10; the Writer writes the full chapter from the research brief.

---

## Chapter 11 Playbook

**Strategy setting with the AI Transformation Framework** belongs in **Activation** (not Foundation). Foundation = governance, data readiness, AI literacy. Activation = strategy setting with the AI Transformation Framework (Product, People, Process, Technology, Data), Centre of Excellence, pilots, first wins. Scale = cross-functional transformation, operating model redesign.

## Case Studies

**Maximum word count per case study:** 1,000 words. Case Study Agent and writers must keep each embedded case study at or below this limit.

**Chapter word targets (chapters that include a case study):** The TOC in `plan/toc.md` gives a word target per chapter. The **Writer** drafts all sections except the case study and must target **TOC chapter word count minus 1,000 words**. The **Case Study Agent** adds up to 1,000 words for the case study. Combined, the chapter hits the TOC target. (Chapters without a case study, e.g. Chapter 10: Writer targets the full TOC word count for that chapter.)
