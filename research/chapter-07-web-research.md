## Findings

### Finding 1
- **Query:** Schneider Electric Philippe Rambach Chief AI Officer AI Hub industrial AI 2024 2025
- **Claim or quote:** Schneider Electric appointed Philippe Rambach as its first Chief AI Officer (CAIO) and established a global AI Hub in November 2021. Rambach drives AI innovation at scale for both internal operations and customer solutions, focusing on efficiency, sustainability, and data-based insights. The AI Hub ensures scalability and measurable value for AI projects, combining technology, process, and human enablers. By 2024, the company employed more than 350 AI experts.
- **Source:** Schneider Electric press release (Newswire.ca); Electrical Industry News Week; Schneider Electric blog
- **URL:** https://www.newswire.ca/news-releases/schneider-electric-advances-its-artificial-intelligence-ai-strategy-with-appointment-of-chief-ai-officer-and-creation-of-new-ai-hub-809611945.html
- **Use for:** Case Study — Schneider Electric (Philippe Rambach, AI Hub structure, CAIO role, 350+ AI experts)

### Finding 2
- **Query:** Schneider Electric Philippe Rambach Chief AI Officer AI Hub industrial AI 2024 2025
