## Findings

### Finding 1
- **Query:** McKinsey AI task completion doubling rate 2024 "every 4 months" agentic organization
- **Claim or quote:** AI task completion ability doubled every 7 months since 2019 and every 4 months since 2024, reaching roughly 2 hours of autonomous work as of late 2025; underlying data comes from METR (accessed September 2025). McKinsey projects AI systems could potentially complete 4 days of work without supervision by 2027.
- **Source:** McKinsey, "The Agentic Organization: Contours of the Next Paradigm for the AI Era," September 2025; METR research, September 2025
- **URL:** https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/people-and-organizational-performance/our-insights/the-agentic-organization-contours-of-the-next-paradigm-for-the-ai-era
- **Use for:** Theme 1 — Scale/speed of AI capability growth (exponential, doubling rates)

### Finding 2
- **Query:** EU Europe AI adoption rate 2025 Eurostat enterprise percentage gap United States investment
- **Claim or quote:** In 2025, 20.0% of EU enterprises with 10 or more employees used AI technologies — up from 13.5% in 2024, a gain of 6.5 percentage points in a single year. Country leaders: Denmark (42%), Finland (37.8%), Sweden (35%). Laggards: Romania (5.2%), Poland (8.4%). US private AI investment reached over $109 billion in 2024 — 24 times the UK figure.
- **Source:** Eurostat, December 2025; European Commission data
- **URL:** https://ec.europa.eu/eurostat/web/products-eurostat-news/w/ddn-20251211-2
- **Use for:** Theme 6 — European context: EU AI adoption rates, investment gap vs US

### Finding 3
- **Query:** EU Europe AI adoption rate 2025 Eurostat; World Economic Forum Europe AI adoption lag
- **Claim or quote:** Europe is lagging in AI adoption. There is a significant gap between large (55% AI adoption) and small enterprises (17% AI adoption) in the EU. The World Economic Forum highlighted that Europe's AI adoption lag risks compounding competitiveness gaps unless leaders act now.
- **Source:** World Economic Forum, September 2025; Eurostat 2025
- **URL:** https://www.weforum.org/stories/2025/09/europe-ai-adoption-lag/
- **Use for:** Theme 6 — European context: adoption gap and urgency for CEO action

### Finding 4
- **Query:** First-mover advantage AI transformation narrowing window 2025 2026 competitive separation leaders laggards
- **Claim or quote:** BCG's "The Widening AI Value Gap" (September 2025) found that future-built companies — the top 5% globally — achieve 1.7x revenue growth, 3.6x three-year total shareholder return (TSR), and 1.6x EBIT margin versus laggards. These leaders plan to spend more than twice as much on AI in 2025 compared to laggards.
- **Source:** BCG, "The Widening AI Value Gap: Build for the Future 2025," September/October 2025
- **URL:** https://www.bcg.com/press/30september2025-ai-leaders-outpace-laggards-revenue-growth-cost-savings
- **Use for:** Theme 5 — Named framework (paradigm shift arc); first-mover advantage narrowing window

### Finding 5
- **Query:** First-mover advantage AI transformation narrowing window 2025 2026 competitive separation leaders laggards
- **Claim or quote:** Globally, only 5% of companies qualify as "future-built" for AI; 35% are "scalers" beginning to generate value; 60% are "laggards" with minimal gains. Agents already account for 17% of total AI value in 2025, expected to reach 29% by 2028. The gap between leaders and laggards is widening, not narrowing.
- **Source:** BCG, "The Widening AI Value Gap," October 2025
- **URL:** https://media-publications.bcg.com/The-Widening-AI-Value-Gap-October-2025.pdf
- **Use for:** Theme 5 — Paradigm shift arc (convergence, divergence, separation); theme 2 — 95% failure/value paradox

### Finding 6
- **Query:** DBS Bank AI transformation Piyush Gupta 2024 S$750 million economic value CEO-led
- **Claim or quote:** DBS Bank generated S$750 million in AI economic value in 2024, up from S$180 million in 2022, with projections exceeding S$1 billion for 2025. The bank has deployed over 1,500 AI/ML models across 370+ distinct use cases. DBS has been named World's Best Bank (Euromoney 2025) and is a case study at Harvard Business School and INSEAD.
- **Source:** DBS Bank annual results; Euromoney Awards for Excellence 2025; MIT Sloan Management Review portrait of Piyush Gupta
- **URL:** https://www.easyglobalbanking.com/singapore-banking/singapore-ai-banking/; https://sloanreview.mit.edu/article/portrait-of-an-ai-leader-piyush-gupta-of-dbs-bank/
- **Use for:** Theme 7 — Case study data: DBS Bank (gold standard CEO-led transformation)

### Finding 7
- **Query:** ING Bank AI transformation 90% pilot production rate agentic 2025 2026 European case study
- **Claim or quote:** ING's centralised AI platform achieves 90% pilot-to-production rate versus a 30% industry average. For mortgage processing, agentic AI handles document extraction and compliance checks; KYC checks that previously took days now take seconds, with 80% of standard data points answered without customer involvement. Agentic AI-generated mortgages planned for 2026 in Germany and the Netherlands.
- **Source:** Computer Weekly, ING interviews, 2025; FFNews, 2025
- **URL:** https://www.computerweekly.com/news/366626743/ING-Bank-transforming-operations-through-agentic-AI
- **Use for:** Theme 7 — Case study data: ING (European alternative to DBS)

### Finding 8
- **Query:** EU AI Act high-risk obligations August 2026 European CEO compliance enterprise impact
- **Claim or quote:** Full compliance obligations for high-risk AI systems take effect 2 August 2026, with penalties up to €35 million or 7% of global annual turnover. Obligations apply regardless of where a company is incorporated — any firm using AI in or affecting EU users must comply. The European Commission's proposed Digital Omnibus could extend the deadline to December 2027, but compliance planning should treat August 2026 as binding.
- **Source:** EU AI Act official text; Orrick, "The EU AI Act: 6 Steps to Take Before 2 August 2026," November 2025; DLA Piper briefing, August 2025
- **URL:** https://www.orrick.com/en/Insights/2025/11/The-EU-AI-Act-6-Steps-to-Take-Before-2-August-2026
- **Use for:** Theme 6 — EU AI Act implications for European CEOs

### Finding 9
- **Query:** BCG AI leaders laggards performance gap total shareholder return 2025 2x ROI separation
- **Claim or quote:** AI leaders achieve 2.7x greater ROI from AI investments compared to laggards (BCG, October 2025). Future-built firms achieve 3.6x three-year TSR. These companies also report double the revenue increase and 40% greater cost reductions in areas where AI is applied.
- **Source:** BCG, "The Widening AI Value Gap," September/October 2025
- **URL:** https://www.bcg.com/publications/2025/are-you-generating-value-from-ai-the-widening-gap
- **Use for:** Theme 2 — failure/value paradox; theme 5 — separation between leaders and followers

### Finding 10
- **Query:** Board governance AI knowledge gap 66% 2025 AI oversight survey
- **Claim or quote:** 66% of board members and C-suite executives say their boards still have "limited to no knowledge or experience" with AI (an improvement from 79% in the prior survey). Only 31% of respondents say AI is not on the board agenda (down from 45%). Deloitte's second global board AI governance pulse survey, 695 respondents across 56 countries.
- **Source:** Deloitte Global, "Governance of AI: A Critical Imperative for Today's Boards, 2nd Edition," 2025
- **URL:** https://www.deloitte.com/global/en/issues/trust/progress-on-ai-in-the-boardroom-but-room-to-accelerate.html
- **Use for:** Theme 4 — Governance vacuum: boards not keeping pace

### Finding 11
- **Query:** Allianz AI transformation 2025 400 GenAI use cases Nemo 80% reduction claim processing
- **Claim or quote:** Allianz has close to 400 GenAI use cases as of December 2024, and Project Nemo — launched in Australia in July 2025 — achieves an 80% reduction in claim processing and settlement time using seven task-specific AI agents. For eligible food spoilage claims under $327, processing time dropped from several days to hours. Launched in under 100 days.
- **Source:** Allianz press release and mediacenter, November 2025; CompleteAI Training news summary
- **URL:** https://www.allianz.com/en/mediacenter/news/articles/251103-when-the-storm-clears-so-should-the-claim-queue.html
- **Use for:** Theme 7 — Case study data: Allianz (European alternative case study)
