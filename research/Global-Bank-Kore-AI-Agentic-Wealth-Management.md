# Kore.ai Agentic Workflow: Global Bank Wealth Management Case Study

## Research Note on Scale

**Requested:** A big US bank implementing agentic workflow with Kore.ai for 18,000 wealth managers.

**Found:** Kore.ai’s main wealth management case study describes a **leading global financial services company** that is **not named**. Published figures are **80,000 wealth advisors** and **120,000 employees**, not 18,000. No public source was found that mentions 18,000 wealth managers in this context.

---

## Overview

A leading global financial services company (unnamed) partnered with Kore.ai to deploy an agentic AI platform that changed how wealth advisors and employees access information and complete tasks. The deployment evolved from basic FAQ automation to an enterprise-grade AI work application built on Kore.ai’s **AI for Work** solution.

**Scale (published figures):**
- **120,000 employees** engaged with the platform
- **80,000 wealth advisors** using the solution
- **80,000 AI interactions daily**
- **2,600+ agents** built by employees using the low-code-to-pro-code builder

---

## The Challenge

- **Fragmented systems:** Advisors spent hours searching across disconnected wealth management platforms and internal databases to answer single client questions.
- **Generic tools insufficient:** Zoom, Microsoft Teams, and generic AI assistants produced only surface-level summaries, unsuitable for client-facing work.
- **Manual work:** Advisors spent hours on meeting notes, research synthesis, market data, and client recommendations.
- **Compliance:** Any solution had to fit the firm’s design system, integrate with existing infrastructure, and meet strict regulatory requirements.

---

## The Solution: Agentic AI Platform

Kore.ai implemented a custom-branded internal AI application with phased rollout:

### Phase 1: Content Creation and Communication
- Email Wizard, Marketing Muse for client communications and marketing
- Summarizer for research digests
- Debrief for meeting transcription and summaries

### Phase 2: Client Management and Meeting Intelligence
- **Meeting Prep:** Consolidates email, CRM, and portfolio data; cuts prep time from 2–3 hours to 15 minutes
- Estate Planning Document Analyzer
- Asset Verification Letters for standardized client correspondence

### Phase 3: Compliance and Governance
- CRC Pre-Review: Evaluates communications against regulatory requirements; reduces review from days to minutes
- Built-in compliance checks before information is shown to advisors

### Phase 4: Portfolio Management and Investment Intelligence
- Financial Advisor Super Agent for portfolios and market insights
- ESG Due Diligence for sustainability criteria
- Investment Advisor for retirement planning
- Deep Research for cross-domain analysis

### Phase 5: Employee Productivity
- Goal-Setting Assistant for performance management

---

## Platform Capabilities

- **Continuous learning:** Accuracy improves with advisor usage and feedback
- **Deep integration:** Connects to wealth platforms, internal databases, conferencing tools, and knowledge repositories without major infrastructure changes
- **Built-in compliance:** All retrieved information passes compliance checks before presentation
- **Natural language interaction:** Advisors ask questions in natural language; agents route requests, retrieve information, or execute tasks
- **Omnichannel access:** Desktop, mobile, voice
