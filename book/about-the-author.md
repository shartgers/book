## About the author

Stephan Hartgers-Rus is a former McKinsey strategy consultant with over 25 years of experience. He helps organisations create tangible value from technology. This work began long before AI reached the boardroom. He studied Econometrics and Computer Science, which are fields now central to the rise of AI.

During his tenure at McKinsey, Stephan led high-stakes transformation programmes. He later held senior leadership roles at the digital agency Mobiquity. He recently became Chief Executive Officer of Xomnia, a Dutch data and AI consultancy. He took the role to help organisations navigate the complexities of AI transformation—where technology, data, people and governance must advance together. In that capacity he leads a team that partners with executive teams to move from isolated experiments to sustained, responsible impact. 

The Agentic Organisation is the distillation of his work in the field. It provides strategic clarity for leaders who understand that transformation happens in the organisation, not the algorithm. Stephan lives in The Hague with his wife and two daughters. He is an avid kite-surfer.
