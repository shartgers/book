4. **The agentic future.** Gartner: 15% of day-to-day work decisions will be made autonomously by 2028 (up from ~0% in 2024); 33% of enterprise software will include agentic AI by 2028. BCG: 250% increase in expected AI decision-making authority within 3 years; 76% of executives already view agents as coworkers. McKinsey: transitions that traditionally took 5–7 years will now take 1–3 years. Source: Gartner 2025; BCG/MIT Sloan 2025; McKinsey 2026.

5. **The Trailblazer profile.** BCG AI Radar (January 2026, 2,360 executives, 640 CEOs): 15% of CEOs are "Trailblazers" who spend 8+ hours/week on own AI upskilling, allocate 60% of AI budgets to workforce upskilling (vs 24–27% for others), and direct >50% of 2026 investment to AI agents. They are 2× as likely to deploy agents end-to-end and see the greatest returns. ~70% are "Pragmatists" (invest when value evident); ~15% are "Followers" (cautious).

## Case Study Data — BBVA

- **Company:** BBVA (Spain). Chair: Carlos Torres Vila.
- **Dilemma:** Whether to continue isolated AI pilots (safe, incremental) or commit to enterprise-wide AI deployment (risky, transformative). Most European banks were still running experiments.
- **Decision made:** Strategic alliance with OpenAI to embed generative AI into core banking operations. Deployed ChatGPT Enterprise across all 120,000 employees in 25 countries. Co-developing AI systems spanning internal productivity, software development, risk analysis, and customer engagement.
- **Implementation:** Created "AI Factory" of 400+ professionals. Placed 300 embedded "AI wizards" in business units. Built "Routing agent" architecture for agentic customer service: specialised agents identify customer intent, differentiate information requests from transactions, and execute banking operations (payments, balance checks). Completed full global cloud migration with a unified data and AI platform.
- **Outcomes:** 80% daily engagement across workforce. Employees saving ~3 hours/week. 3,000+ custom bots created. Enterprise-wide deployment rather than isolated pilots.
- **Torres Vila quote:** "We were pioneers in the digital and mobile transformation, and we are now entering the AI era with even greater ambition."
- **Transferable lesson:** The leadership decision to stop piloting and go enterprise-wide, backed by infrastructure investment (cloud, data platform) and workforce enablement (AI wizards, ChatGPT Enterprise for all), is the difference between the 5% who achieve value and the 95% who don't.
